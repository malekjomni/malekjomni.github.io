Greed Island: Cooperative Multiplayer Mobile Action Game

As an academic project during my studies, I collaborated with a group of four individuals to develop "Greed Island," a cooperative multiplayer mobile action game. This endeavor allowed us to apply theoretical knowledge into practice, emphasizing teamwork and collective problem-solving. From conceptualizing gameplay mechanics to coding and testing, each team member played a vital role in the successful creation of this engaging mobile gaming experience.
