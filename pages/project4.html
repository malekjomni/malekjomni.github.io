Virtual Restaurant Experience | VR Mini Game Prototype

Explore the immersive 'VR Restaurant Simulator,' a personal project where you can play as a staff member washing dishes and preparing tables, or switch roles to become a client placing orders and enjoying virtual dining. This ongoing project, driven by my passion for virtual experiences, is continuously evolving with upcoming features, including cooking simulations, to enhance the gaming adventure

Contributions and Achievements:

Dish Cleaning Tool

The primary tool for efficiently cleaning and maintaining dishes in the kitchen.

Scrubbing Procedure

Utilize the sponge to scrub and clean dishes effectively

Dish Washing Process

Rinse dishes under tap water to thoroughly wash away any residue.

Guided Task Panel

An interactive panel guiding you through various kitchen tasks, ensuring a systematic and efficient workflow.

Chair Placement

Grab a chair and seamlessly snap it into its designated socket

Table Cloth Placement

Place a white piece of fabric on the table.

Napkin Placement

Properly place napkins in their designated sockets, adding a touch of elegance to the dining setup.

Interactive Table

A table set with a question mark, indicating interactive elements. Users can interact to sit down and explore.

Handheld Menu

Access the restaurant menu through an interactive handheld menu, allowing users to explore and place orders.

Fork Dining Experience

Enjoy a plated meal using a knife and fork for a sophisticated and delightful dining experience.

Spoon Dining Experience

Savor a delicious dish using a spoon for a convenient and enjoyable dining experience.

Burger Delight

Indulge in a burger plated for a delightful dining experience.
