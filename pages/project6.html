Empire of the Future: Multiplayer Resource Management RTS Game

As a freelance developer, I had the opportunity to work on Empire of the Future, a multiplayer RTS game where players build and manage their own empires, capture resource nodes, and engage in trade and battles for dominance. I was responsible for the entire development process, from gameplay mechanics and multiplayer systems to UI design and implementation.

In terms of gameplay, I designed systems that allow players to capture and upgrade resource nodes, which are crucial for gathering the resources needed to expand their empire. The multiplayer aspect involved real-time player interactions, enabling users to trade resources, form alliances, and compete on a global leaderboard.

I also developed a resource hunt event, a Battle Royale-style mini-game where players race against time, navigating a map filled with power-ups and traps to collect as many resources as possible. The UI was crafted to ensure an intuitive and seamless player experience, enhancing overall engagement.

This project showcases my expertise in end-to-end game development, multiplayer systems, and UI design, while delivering an immersive and strategic RTS experience.
